When emailing about a bug…

A few details make a huge difference for figuring things out quickly:

  • Your macOS version (Apple menu → About This Mac)
  • Your Mac model (also under About This Mac)
  • Plugr version (Plugr menu → About Plugr)
  • What you were doing when the problem happened
  • What you expected, and what actually happened
  • Screenshots if relevant (⌘⇧4 to capture an area)
