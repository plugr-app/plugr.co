Pricing questions

More general questions on the FAQ page.

Can I switch between tiers later?

Yes. Monthly → Annual upgrades anytime, prorated. Subscription → Lifetime is also fine; we credit any time remaining on your current sub against the Lifetime price. Lifetime → anything is a refund situation — email us and we'll work it out.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You keep access until the end of your current billing period, then Plugr drops to read-only mode. Your existing scan, tags, favorites, and project library all stay visible — you just can't run new scans, check updates, or use paid-only features until you resubscribe.

The Deals tab stays fully accessible even in read-only mode, with ownership matching against your last scan.

How does the device limit work?

When you enter your license on a Mac, that Mac counts as one activation. You can deactivate any Mac from your customer portal (link in your purchase email) and re-activate on a different one — useful when you get a new computer or do a clean install.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes — 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked, for any reason. Full details on the refund policy page.

Student / educator discount?

Not at launch. If you're a music technology educator using Plugr in class, email hello@plugr.co and we'll figure something out for your students.

Still on the fence?

The 14-day free trial is the easiest way to decide. No card, no commitment. If it doesn't change how you think about your library, just stop — you keep your data, no nags.
