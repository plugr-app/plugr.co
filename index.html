Frequently asked

Quick answers. Full FAQ here, or email us if you don't see your question.

What plugin formats does Plugr support?

VST3, Audio Unit (AU), VST2, AAX, and CLAP. It also catalogs all the standalone apps on your Mac so you can organize and keep them up to date as well.

Does Plugr work on Windows?

Not yet. Plugr is currently macOS-only (macOS 12 Monterey or later, Apple Silicon and Intel both). We hope to support Windows in the future, but it isn't currently planned.

How accurate is the update checking?

For the hundreds of developers Plugr knows about — most of the big names you use every day — very accurate. For developers it doesn't recognize yet, Plugr's built-in tools will help find the correct update source, or allow you to manually add them as needed, then remembers for next time. With a little bit of one-time setup, Plugr becomes a reliable one-stop update machine.

Several developers (Plugin Alliance, Native Instruments, Slate Digital, Waves, and others) handle updates through their own update managers. Plugr knows about those too, and points you to the right place to grab the update, organizing all your companion apps in one central location.

Will Plugr make permanent changes to my computer?

Only when you explicitly tell it to. "Move to Trash" is reversible (files go to your macOS Trash, not deleted permanently) and asks for confirmation. Everything else — categories, tags, favorites — is metadata that lives in Plugr's local cache and never touches your actual plugin files.

What's the difference between subscription and lifetime?

Subscription gives you all features as long as you're subscribed; cancel and the app drops to a read-only view of your existing scan data. Lifetime is a one-time payment for all features available at the time of purchase, plus all updates to those features forever. We may sunset the Lifetime tier at some point — anyone who bought a lifetime license keeps it forever.

Does Plugr send my data anywhere?

Your library, projects, tags, favorites and notes stay on your Mac, and your additions sync between your Macs if you turn on the iCloud sync feature. Plugr only reaches out to developer websites to check for updates and to the deal sites to pull in sales — it never uploads your library anywhere.

If you turn on iCloud sync, your data syncs between your own Macs through Apple's iCloud, not our servers. Full details in the privacy policy.
